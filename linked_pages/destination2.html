Jammu and Kashmir

Key destinations are Vaishno Devi Temple, Surinsar and Mansar Lake, Dal and Nagin Lake, Mughal Gardens, Amarnath, etc.

Punjab

Punjab are dotted with numerous ancient monuments, gurudwaras, temples, serene lakes with many tourist attractions like Golden Temple, Wagah Border, Jallianwalah Bagh, sheesh mahal , Sukhna lake, etc.

Haryana

Haryana is renowned for its ancient temples, impressive architecture and lush greenery, with Key tourist attractions- Kurukshetra, Panipat, Sultanpur bird sanctuary, Dumdama Lake, etc.

Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh proves to be a wonderful location for who want to relax and simply feel refresh with numerous hill stations and Key tourist attractions- Chamba valley, Spiti valley, Shimla, Manali, and Kullu etc.

Uttarakhand

The Land of Gods, owing to numerous pilgrimage centres and Hindu temples throughout the state. Key destinations are Nanital, Mussoorie, Rishikesh, Haridwar, Gangotri, yamunotri, kedarnath, Badrinath, etc.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is a unique combination of royal and tribal India with many historical places amd monuments some destinations are Amer Fort, Hawa Mahal and Jal Mahal, City Palace, Lake Palace, Chittorgarh etc.

Uttar Pradesh

UP is home to numerous historical monuments and places of religious significance. Major tourist attractions are Agra, prayagraj, Sarnath, jhansi, Varanasi, Ayodhya, Vrindavan, etc.

Bihar

Bihar is dotted with historical monuments, Buddhist monasteries and many religious places. key travel attractions are Nalanda, Rajgir, Bodh Gaya, Patna,Vaishali, Vikramshila, and Pawapuri, etc.

Gujrat

Gujarat is one of the most vibrant coastal states of India, rich in crafts, history and natural beauty. Ahmedabad, Sabarmati Ashram, Dwarka, Somnath Temple, and Porbandar are some tourist attractions.

Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh have immense natural beauty and splendour, wildlife sanctuaries and national parks. Tourist attractions are Khajuraho, Panna and Kanha National Park, Karera Bird Sanctuary, Ujjain etc.

Jharkhand

Jharkhand is known for its rich mineral wealth, beauty of forests, amazing wild life, and waterfalls. key attractions are Tagore Hill, Kanke Dam, War Cemetery, Betla National Park, Hazaribagh Sanctuary, etc.

West Bengal

West Bengal is the of many crumbling British Raj-era gems, beautiful gardens, and historical institutes. Key attractions are B.B.D. Bagh, Victoria Memorial, Darjeeling, Sundarbans and the Howrah Bridge, etc.

Goa

Goa is known for its palm-fringed beaches, culinary delights, and lively parties. Key attractions are Calangute Beach, Agauda Fort, Manguesh temple, Anjuna Beach, Colva beach, Dona Paula, etc.

Maharashtra

Maharashtra is dotted with numerous forts, caves, beaches, hill stations, historical monuments. Key tourist attractions are Mumbai, Elephanta, Ajanta, Ellore Caves, Ganapati phule, and Shirdi, etc.

Karnataka

Karnataka, the land of ultimate natural beauty and rich cultural heritage blessed with roaring Jog waterfalls, densely forested Sahayadri, and the rich wildlife sanctuaries like Nagarhole and Bandipur.

Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is home to many famous hill stations, wild life sanctuaries, national parks and, virgin beaches. The major tourist attractions are Asamgarh Palace, Char Minar, Bhongir Fort , Belum Caves, etc.

Kerela

This exotic land is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, tropical climate, palm fringed beaches. Key tourist attractions- Aattukal Bhagavathi and Padmanabham Temples, Kerala Backwaters, Bekal Fort, Kovalam beach etc.

Tamil Nadu

Tamilnadu have glorious past, vibrant culture, rich history, and natural bounty in the form of beaches. Major destinations are Nilgiris, Ooty, Coonoor, Kotagiri, Doddabetta, and Mukurti Peak. etc.

Sikkim

Sikkim is notable destination for travel enthusiast simply for the gurgling streams, snowcapped mountains, flora and fauna. Key attractions are Gangtok, Nauthu la, Kanchenjunga etc.

Meghalaya

Meghalaya is picturesque states, The panoramic landscapes, foggy hills which serves beauty and tranquility. Key tourist attractions are Uniam Lake, Kyllang Rock, Lady Hydari park,, Mini zoo, Shillong peak, etc.

Manipur

Manipur, popularly referred to as the 'Switzerland of India'. key attraction are Loktak Lake, Sendra Island, Shree Govindajee Temple, Keibul Lamjao National Park, Moirang, Kaina, etc.
