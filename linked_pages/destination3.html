Daman & Diu

Daman & Diu is the most popular weekend gateway for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts key destinations are Fort of St. Jerome, Devka beach, Zampa Gateway, Gangeshwar temple, Nagoa beach, etc.

Lakshadweep

Lakshadweep is known for its lush green landscape, incredible marine life, soft silvery beaches and water sports. Key attractions are Mosques of Kavaratti, Tuna Canning Factory, Agatti. etc.
